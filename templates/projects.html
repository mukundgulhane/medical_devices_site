P1: Smart Wearable Vital Sign Monitor

Clinical Application & Overview

A low-power, continuous monitoring wearable for real-time tracking of patient **vital signs** (Heart Rate, SpO2, and Body Temperature). The device prioritizes data security and uses a robust wireless protocol to communicate with an external medical data platform, supporting remote patient monitoring (RPM).
