Pulse & Foot Pressure Measurement Circuits

Pulse Measurement

Purpose: Measure the heart rate by detecting blood flow in a fingertip.

  • LED (Red/Infrared): Sends light into the fingertip.
  • Photodiode/Phototransistor: Detects reflected light, converts to voltage signal.
  • Op-Amp: Amplifies small voltage signal.
  • Microcontroller: Reads signal, calculates BPM.
  • LCD/Display: Shows heart rate value.
Mechanism: Light changes with blood volume ‚Üí photodiode detects ‚Üí amplified ‚Üí filtered ‚Üí counted ‚Üí display BPM.

Foot Pressure Measurement

Purpose: Measure foot pressure using Force Sensitive Resistor (FSR).

  • FSR / Pressure Sensor: Resistance decreases as pressure increases.
  • Voltage Divider: Converts resistance to voltage signal.
  • Op-Amp (optional): Amplifies voltage signal.
  • Microcontroller: Reads analog voltage and converts to pressure.
  • LCD/Display: Shows foot pressure value.
Mechanism: Foot pressure ‚Üí FSR resistance changes ‚Üí voltage signal ‚Üí amplification/ADC ‚Üí display pressure.
